How the modern technology helps you in your chosen field of study now?

by: Mainit Jerson D.

Technology is everywhere; in our house; there are televisions, laptops, smartphones, and even game consoles. In the street, there is closed-circuit television (CCTV) to help the law enforcer prevent or detect crime. It is becoming more prevalent as human technology advances.

Being a student of computer science technology is extremely beneficial to us. Technology makes student life easier when looking for the information needed. You just need to search for the details, and the internet will provide you with the answers to your questions. Simply read the information provided carefully; not everything on the internet is true.

Even in the midst of a pandemic, technology can benefit a student. To continue our studies, we can study and meet virtually using Microsoft Teams and Google Classroom.

There are sites that I know will help you in your self-study.

  • Youtube

     » it is a site where you can watch tutorials on every topic you need. There are tutorials for English, math, science, games, and many more.

  • w3School

     » it is a free site that helps computer science students or even a career shifters to study basic programming languages.

  • FreeCodeCamp

     » is a free website that teaches you some programming languages and gives you a certificate after you finish the course. You will not just only read here you need to pass the task given at the end of the course to have a certificate.

  • Udemy

     » it is a free and paid website that helps you in your self-study. It is free because some of its discussion is short. You can have paid tutorials with complete materials and a certificate after you finish a course you choose. It is a good option if you are in need of a certificate, some companies in other county accept this certificate. You just need to sign-up on this website to learn more.

Technology helps us with everything we need, but too much of it will lead us to addiction and lessen our social communication with others. We must use technology wisely and effectively.
